This was a two person project and our first Object Oriented Program. We made the most of APIE with class members organinc and robotic cats and dogs. In Doggos you run a virtual pet shelter where animals needs must be met and your roster of current animals living at the shelter must be managed. Animals can be taken in and adopted out and are randomly generated from predifined lists of names and breeds as well as the animals quirks; likes and dislike's; favorite activities and toys ect.
