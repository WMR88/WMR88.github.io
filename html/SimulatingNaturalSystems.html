Preface: Random Walk's:

A Random Walk is simply a a path defined by a series of random steps. Here is a simple 8-directional random walk. At each step we color the pixel at the
walkers location black before the next step is calculated by chosing to increment or decrement the walkers X and Y position.
Random walker's can be used to model real world phenomena such as the movement of molocules in a gas, probablitity in card games, and is even utilized
in finance.



Preface: Distribution of numbers: Random Distribution
A pseudo-random distribution of numbers, achived by simply using most launguage librarys random() function will generaly return a close to even
distribution of numbers. On this canvas we will pick random numbers between 1 and 10 from javascript's Math library, and count how many times
each number is picked.

Use the "Run" button multiple time's to view new sets of randomly distributed number.
